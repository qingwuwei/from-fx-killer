Japan's Finance Minister Satsuki Katayama expressed her concerns on Wednesday regarding the recent one-sided and rapid fluctuations in the currency markets. Katayama highlighted her commitment to closely monitor foreign exchange rates, signaling a heightened sense of urgency amid these turbulent conditions.

The yen has been under pressure against major currencies, including the USD and EUR, leading to significant implications for Japan's economy and trade balances. Traders are closely watching the USD/JPY pair, which has shown increased volatility, with levels surpassing key psychological thresholds. As the government remains vigilant, market participants are left to navigate potential interventions and policy adjustments that may arise in response to these erratic trading patterns.
